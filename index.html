About Us

JEM's Speakers Club is a non-governmental organization dedicated to promoting open speaking and building a supportive environment for people to find and implement sustainable solutions to vital issues in society.

Our Vision

"To build a community that promotes the unfolding of secrets, problem-solving, and implementation of sustainable solutions to neglected, sensitive, and vital issues in society."

Our Mission

"To use various creative and developmental activities and programs to motivate and educate people as well as build a supportive environment for them to feel free and/or be close enough to speak openly, be intrinsically motivated, and uphold patriotism, whilst supporting the underprivileged in society."

Our Activities

We engage in a variety of activities to achieve our mission:

Writings

  • Shairi Fikiri: Thought-provoking poems on contemporary issues.
  • Projection Poems: Poems promoting essential social, cultural, and political issues.
  • Inking Heart: Deep reflection poems revealing inner truths.

Shows

  • TOBox: Youth opinions on education, health, arts, culture, and politics.
  • Brief Talks: Inspires society on creativity and innovation.
  • Set Talks: Motivational and inspirational program.

Programs

  • Youth-Cry: A poetic show on youth trauma.
  • Vijana-Concerns: Youth SWOT analysis forum.
  • Speakers Nobel Prize: Awards for exceptional qualities.
